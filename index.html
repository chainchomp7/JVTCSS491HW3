Collision Interaction. "Enemies" Follow closely towards the "leader" Jerry, the gray-haired boy.
When Jerry touches an enemy's inner box (Dino, Snowman, Duck) while "alive", he will become knocked back and become a ghost, and his speed will increase.
As a ghost, Jerry cannot be knocked back in this state, though enemies will still attempt to follow. Enemies' larger outer boxes will attempt to make other
enemies be pushed away from each other. "Healer" components (small boy and 3 top-like machines) will restore him back if he touches one.
All objects' outer boxes are pushed away from walls. Healers can go through other objects besides walls.
Outer boxes are green. Enemy inner boxes are red. Jerry and Healers' boxes are blue.
